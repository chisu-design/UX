CatFinder

CatFinder is a web app that allows people to share their location on cats they come across and upload cat pictures.


Project Statement

I am a huge fan of cats. I notice them a lot in my neighborhood and love seeing them. Sometime, I want to know what cats has been spotted in the area and what are the chances of me coming across them or if I recognize a particular one. I decided to create this web app for this purpose to let people upload random cats they saw on the street. I hope that more people use this app so users will know about the cats around them. I also hope that it can also help people find their lost cat as another benefit of using this.


Wireframe

Code

I used Firebase, a Backend-as-a-Service that allows me to store data on the cloud. The database contains images, information on the cat name, and GPS coordinates. I used HTML5, CSS3, and jQuery to make the layout a functionality. It has a responsive layout based on what device the user is using.
Please turn on location service when using the app.
